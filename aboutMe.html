About The Web Designer In Calgary

Hey There!

My Name is Justin Shaw. I was born and raised here in Calgary, Alberta Canada. Ever since 2016, I have been involved in computer coding. I started learning python in school and moved on to HTML and CSS the following year. I really enjoyed this and decided I would go to school for it and I fell in love even more than before building and designing websites. This passion has brought me here today, starting my own website and freelancing business.

With my love for web designing, I wanted to give people the ability to have the website of their dreams for whatever they want it for. I'll build anything you wish for with 110% of my abilities. I am always learning new tricks and coming up with new ideas to implement into websites.

When I'm not working on any new projects or client websites. I'm at home with my cat, playing games with my friends, or hanging out with the person I love most. I like to ride my motorbike wherever I need to in the summer and visit nature's best destination within a day trip.
